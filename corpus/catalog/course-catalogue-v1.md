## Philosophy — The Two-Sided AI Filter

Every course earns its place on one of two grounds:

1. **AI cannot do this** — genuinely human, real-time, relational work: holding a room, reading a person, having the hard conversation, being answerable for a call
2. **AI makes this matter more** — when AI generates the options, analysis, and first draft, the scarce thing left is judgment

The second bucket is the stronger marketing frame: it speaks directly to "will this replace me?" anxiety and flips it into an offer.

## Course Generation Engine — Four Axes

Each competency is run through an engine with four axes:
- **Stages of the moment:** Prepare, open, navigate, close, follow up
- **Scenarios:** Same skill in a different room (boss, peer, client, subordinate)
- **Failure modes:** What goes wrong, how you recover
- **Role lens:** Employee, manager, entrepreneur facing the same skill differently

## Differentiators

1. **Anchored to established frameworks** — ICF, ATD, Harvard, Cynefin, Kahneman, SHRM BASK, CCL
2. **African corporate localization** — hierarchy, age/seniority, relationship-first business, pan-African spread (Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, South Africa)
3. **Every course ends in proof** — capstone artifact built on a real situation, not just a certificate of attendance
