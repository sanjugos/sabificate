### 2. Accreditation Strategy (All)
- **Core tension:** NCCE is the accrediting body — can they accredit their own CPD?
- **Mark's approach:** Use "affiliated with" language, lean on advisory board of PhDs, honest about being new
- **Gbitse's prior precedent:** 10 years ago, signed MOU with a monotechnic (same NCCE contact as proprietor), certificates issued in monotechnic's name, 10% revenue share, ran 3 years
- **Path forward:** Gbitse to approach Father George (Catholic University of Nigeria connection) for affiliation
- **Mark's insight:** "Accreditation is them lending us their credibility" — affiliated/partnered is the soft-touch version
- **NCCE self-accreditation framing:** They accredit others, so for their own CPD, they could put it through their own Director of Accreditation process
