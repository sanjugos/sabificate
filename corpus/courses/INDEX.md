## Key Strategic Insights from Research

1. **ITF reimbursement gap**: Only ~580 of tens of thousands of liable employers claim ITF training reimbursement — SABIficate's most powerful B2B sales lever
2. **DNFBP compliance gap**: DNFBPs filed only 38 SARs in 2024 vs 19,873 from banks — massive underserved market
3. **CIBN digital CPD window**: CIBN's e-learning platform is nascent while IMPACT Agenda 2026-2028 mandates digital CPD delivery
4. **NBA MCPD uncertainty**: Federal High Court ruled CPD cannot be tied to licence renewal (Jan 2026) — risk factor but also teaching opportunity
5. **NIIRA 2025 urgency**: Brand-new insurance legislation with July 2026 recapitalization deadline driving industry-wide retraining
6. **CBN AI mandate**: March 2026 CBN directive requiring AI/ML in all banks creates immediate training demand
7. **Nigeria EI amplifier**: Hofstede data (PDI 80/IDV 30) shows EI has amplified impact in Nigerian vs Western organizational contexts
8. **Employee retention crisis**: 71% Nigerian employee resignation rate within 12 months; Wema Bank's 112% salary increase turnaround
9. **Pension opportunity**: PenOp training catalog heavily weighted toward soft skills — structured regulatory content gap for SABIficate
10. **CBN consumer protection enforcement**: 50x increase in penalties (N18.8B total) creates urgent training demand
