## Key Themes Discussed

### 1. Curriculum & Pedagogy
- Problem-driven microlearning (not 2-hour replicated classroom content)
- Adaptive difficulty: beginner/intermediate/advanced adjusting language dynamically
- Example: "EBITDA" for advanced vs "revenue minus expenses" for beginners
- Test-out capability: if learner demonstrates mastery, skip ahead and earn certificates
- Project-oriented: learners build toward a portfolio/capstone, not just consume content
- AI evaluates submitted artifacts and gives feedback (replaces human grading at scale)
- Graphically rich content for varying literacy levels

### 2. Technology Architecture
- No Articulate Storyline — too expensive, requires contracts/servers
- AI generates interactive HTML/JS content directly (demonstrated with rock collection database example)
- Web-first with PWA/app wrapper for mobile presence

- Sanju's subject matter expertise feeds AI content generation; he reviews/approves output
